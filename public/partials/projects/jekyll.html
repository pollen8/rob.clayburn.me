The Project

Slingshot wanted to create an experience using technology and historic resources based around the book "The Strange Case of Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde" Players should experience the cult novel in a new and innovative environment, transcending characters, themes and narrative to reveal, and release, their inner Hyde.

My work

I was charged with taking a Zephyr bio harness's biometric data, routed through an electric imp, to control the playback of the game's HTML5 video and audio sources. This enabled the game player to play, pause and restart videos by holding their breaths.
