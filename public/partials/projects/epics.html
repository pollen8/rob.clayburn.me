EPICS

The project

EADTU is Europe's leading institutional association for open and distance higher education, and is at the heart of the modernisation agenda of European universities.

EADTU has a membership of fifteen institutions and fourteen national associations across 25 nations. Its membership covers over 200 universities and around 3 million students.

In conjunction with Futuron, I was approached to build a European Portal for International Courses and Services (EPICS). Its aim was to enable students to find international courses within the mainstream offerings of their university.

My work
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • PHP

Using Fabrik, we provided an administration section for universities to add and manage their courses. Students were then able to search these courses or browse via a tagging system.
