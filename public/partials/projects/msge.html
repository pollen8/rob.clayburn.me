MSGE is a multi channel realtime group chat server which I developed for Slingshot effect to help them manage comminication between teams of players during their various street games.

Communication is achieved via SMS messages, emails and tweets, so you can 'join' a game by texting 'join gameName', or if there are teams you can 'join team Name'.
