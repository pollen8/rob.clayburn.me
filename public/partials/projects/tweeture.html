Tweeture

The project

The UK Arts Council commissioned a group of companies to create a social media experience to capture the imagination of SXSW festival attendants and promote Bristol as a technology hub.

The team brainstormed the concept of the Tweeture; a carry-around monster that likes to meet new people. He tweets his whereabouts and how he is being treated by those who pick him up, allowing his new friends to accrue friendship points if they take good care of him.

My work
  • GPS
  • Webservices
  • PHP
  • Twitter API

My role was to collect the data stream from the Tweeture's various input devices (accelerometer, GPS, light sensor), along with any tweets that were sent by players to the tweeture. This data was then mapped to the Tweeture's personality resulting in a series of responses sent via the Tweetures's Twitter account.

I also managed the scoring system, which showed daily and overall rankings of how well the player's took care of the Tweeture.

To see the Tweeture in action take a look at the BBC coverage on vimeo.
